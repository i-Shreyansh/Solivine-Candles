About Solivine

Made with Love in Bareilly

At Solivine, we believe in the power of natural fragrances to transform spaces and uplift spirits. Each candle is carefully handcrafted in Bareilly, Uttar Pradesh, using premium soy wax and the finest essential oils.


Our mission is to bring warmth, comfort, and beautiful aromas into your home. Every candle is made with love, attention to detail, and a commitment to quality that you can see and smell.


Our Goal

To create sustainable, eco-friendly candles that not only illuminate your space but also create memorable sensory experiences. We're dedicated to traditional craftsmanship while embracing modern design aesthetics.
